23 November 2020

ArcaneDefence updated:

  • The chef's food cart can no longer store an infinite amount of food stuff.

ArcaneMusic updated:

  • The Nanodrug Plus now correctly deconstructs and can be refilled by it's own refill.
  • The NanoDrug Plus vendor now has a unique coat of paint to distinguish it from the NanoMed Plus.
  • Dirt graves now sound like dirt being shoveled in/out when being closed up by hand.
  • Hydroponics trays growing replica pod seeds will now correctly harvest into seeds if depowered when there's nobody being cloned..
  • False walls will only flash their opening/closing animations once when being toggled, as opposed to being a glitchy nightmare amalgam when pressed.

Fikou updated:

  • moves dough to newfood

Ghommie updated:

  • Fixed pocket spaghetti.
  • Fixed the arena shuttle not being unlockable if medals are disabled.

Jared-Fogle updated:

  • You will no longer get a warning when checking your mood with telekinesis.
  • You can no longer quick swap another pair of glasses with nearsighted to see perfectly.

XDTM updated:

  • Nanite machinery's screens now turn off if they're broken or are unpowered, and are visible through darkness when on.

dragomagol updated:

  • ghosts are no longer able to empty gibbers

21 November 2020

EdgeLordExe updated:

  • No more shittery with inaccessible nodes getting merged.
  • Fixes heretic brews being permanent.
  • Fixes void storm breaking after resurrecting.

Ghommie updated:

  • Being species-swapped to a species without reagent metabolism (such as android and skeleton) will set you free of any chemical addiction your previously fleshy body may have had.
  • Mk-honk banana shoes can now be refilled with bananium even if not on help intent.

Timberpoes updated:

  • Nanotrasen bounty boards no longer leave behind 95% of the machine when tasked with selling one! (Special thanks to ArcaneMusic)
  • The BSA will no longer refund all of its component parts and circuitboard when constructed.
  • Xenomorph drones and higher will once again generate plasma, allowing them to use abilities and evolve once more.
  • Things with the Xenomorph plasma vessel organ will once again heal on alien weeds.

XDTM updated:

  • Added Nanite Storage Protocols, unlockable through B.E.P.I.S. research. They can modify nanite max volume, increasing the cap or sacrificing some for extra replication rate.
  • Removed the Nanite Tinker Replication Protocol.
  • Added the Nanite Pyramid Replication Protocol to replace it. It increases replication rate if the volume is above 80% of the maximum.
  • Renamed the Nanite Offline Production Protocol into Eclipse Protocol. Functionality unchanged.
  • The Chain Detonation nanite program now scales more gradually in power with nanite volume.

itseasytosee updated:

  • The hydrogen ax is now visible in both hands

20 November 2020

ArcaneMusic updated:

  • Hardsuit Thermal regulators will tell you if your cell is keeping you warm, if the internal cell is dead, or when the cell turns itself off.

Caldony updated:

  • The sect of the Ever-Burning Candle has been officially recognised by NT and can now be practiced legally in the chapel.

EdgeLordExe updated:

  • fixes the cursed heart being unable to track people inside containers.
  • fixes influence vision getting broken by replacing someone's eyes.

Ghommie updated:

  • You can now put stuff in someone else's hand through the stripping menu again.
  • Admins now have the option to remove a language from the blocked_languages list when granting it to a mob through the language menu.
  • Fixed a 18.18% chance of gibs streaking 50 tiles away... by canonizing it into an intentional 3% chance upon the request of the bunch of you on github.
  • You can't defy lack of gravity through the power of love.

Ryll/Shaps updated:

  • Plasmamen who are gulag'd via the teleporter with non-plasmaman gloves will now be given a free pair of basic plasmaman gloves so they don't die.

ThePotato97 updated:

  • Rotating stamps on paper by holding shift and moving your mouse.
  • Fixed chat message for stamping paper.

qustinnus updated:

  • instruments work again

tralezab updated:

  • AIs can now select art from the gallery as their display!

wesoda25 updated:

  • You can now craft ladders using 15 rods. Note: crafted ladders can only be connected to other crafter ladders.
